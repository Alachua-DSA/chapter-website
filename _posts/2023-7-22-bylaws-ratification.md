Alachua County DSA voted to approve our [bylaws](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1TT3d4M_FefYt7-fyzs0DN3Jt0DH8FSxG/view?usp=sharing) and officially become a chapter in August. 


Our bylaws are a living document, built with love and care by the steering committee. We will be having an upcoming chapter convention to get more input from the membership on the bylaws and how we run our chapter.
